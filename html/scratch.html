Why I love the FIFA World Cup 2022


  1. It was a fairy tale story
    • It would be Lionel Messi's final attempt at winning the World Cup.
    • Messi had lost World Cup final 2014 against Germany.
    • Argentina had lost early in the tournament, so they had to win every game from the beggining
  2. "Se lo merece Messi" (Messi deserved it)
    • Messi had been told his entire career that he would never be the greatest without winning an international trolphy.
    • Messi took an average team through this tournament by leading in goals scored and assists.
    • Messi did this at the advanced age of 35.
  3. I support the Americas
    • Argentina got their revenge on France from the 2018 World Cup in Russia
    • South America has been deemed as less by Europeans in the world of Football
    • South American footballers are the ones responsible for making football beautiful to me
